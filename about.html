About

Kasa Interiors by Aditi Parkhi.

Owner

Thoughtful luxury, led by material sensitivity and real-world execution.

Aditi Parkhi founded Kasa Interiors to create spaces that feel timeless, immersive, and deeply intuitive to live in. Her work combines luxury design language with practical spatial planning, ensuring every project feels elevated without becoming performative.

With years of experience across residences, commercial projects, turnkey construction, and visualization-led design, Aditi approaches each commission with equal attention to atmosphere, detailing, movement, and usability. Her studio’s dark luxury sensibility is expressed through warm metals, rich textures, balanced proportions, and tailored lighting.

For clients, the process feels collaborative and clear. For spaces, the result feels composed, lasting, and distinctly personal.

Studio Story

Kasa Interiors operates as a design studio that stays closely involved from concept direction to execution decisions. The goal is not only to make spaces beautiful, but to make them legible, efficient, and emotionally resonant.

“We design interiors that hold presence quietly, age beautifully, and become part of the way you live.”

The studio is based in Pune and works across homes, offices, boutique environments, and turnkey transformations where design intent must remain intact through every layer of delivery.

Core Values

The principles behind every Kasa Interiors decision.

Craftsmanship

Precision in joinery, finishing, proportion, and the small visual alignments that define a premium space.

Collaboration

Active partnership with clients, vendors, and site teams so the vision remains intact through delivery.

Integrity

Honest communication around scope, budgets, materials, and execution realities from the very beginning.

Innovation

Fresh ideas in layout, lighting, visualization, and material layering that stay grounded in purpose.

Timelessness

Design choices that age with grace and continue to feel relevant long after trends pass.

Excellence
